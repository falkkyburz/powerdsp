First step: Replace the s with the bilinear formula and multiply numerator and denominator with the highest power of (1 + z^-1)^(highest power of s): b1*K*(1 - z^-1) + b0*(1 + z^-1) H(z) = ----------------------------— a1*K*(1 - z^-1) + a0*(1 + z^-1)

Third Step: Sort the terms: (b0 - b1*K)*z^-1 + (b0 + b1*K) H(z) = ---------------------------— (a0 - a1*K)*z^-1 + (a0 + a1*K)
